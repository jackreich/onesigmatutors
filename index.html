About One Sigma Tutors

Built from my own climb up the curve.

I did my undergrad at Northeastern and have worked in a range of engineering and manufacturing roles since. That path eventually led me to apply to grad school.

Along the way I struggled with the GMAT and GRE, and One Sigma Tutors grew out of how I answered that. I built a study system that got me to my target score and into MIT and other top graduate programs. That experience taught me that improving a score is about far more than test content. It is equally about time management, structured study plans, and preventing mistakes.

I first learned this in high school, where strong tutoring moved my scores from the 65th to the 99th percentile. The inspiration is right there in the name. One sigma means one standard deviation, and I want to help students improve their scores by more than that.
